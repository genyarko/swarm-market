About
Circle (NYSE: CRCL) is a global financial technology firm that enables businesses of all sizes to harness the power of digital currencies and public blockchains for payments, commerce, and financial applications worldwide. Circle is building the world’s largest, most-widely used, stablecoin network, and issues, through its regulated affiliates, USDC and EURC stablecoins. 
Arc is the purpose-built L1 blockchain from Circle and serves as the Economic OS for the internet — powering the programmable trust layer and transaction engine where capital, humans, and machines coordinate to drive real economic growth.  

Together, Nanopayments and Arc unlock a new economic primitive for the internet: programmable value that is finally viable at high frequency. 

Key benefits for developers:
• Process sub-cent transactions on-chain, gas-free, with full control of funds
• Align pricing precisely with usage — no margin lost to gas fees
• Charge per API call, per query, per millisecond of compute, or per action

Agentic Economy in action:
• APIs monetize every request
• AI agents pay for data and services in real time
• Compute marketplaces priced by usage
• Machine-to-machine commerce without intermediaries

The internet made information programmable.

Circle Nanopayments + Arc make value programmable — and economically viable.


Challenge
Build the Agentic Economy on Arc using USDC and Nanopayments.



Your mission is to design and ship an application, agent, or developer tool that leverages programmable USDC and Nanopayments for economically viable transactions to unlock new economic models for APIs, AI Agents, marketplaces, or machine-to-machine commerce.



You’ll choose one of the tracks below (or naturally align to one) and submit a working demo plus a clear explanation of your use case and feedback. 
You must align your project with at least one track. Winners are selected based on the best overall solutions, regardless of track.

All submissions must: 
•  Demonstrate real per-action pricing (≤ $0.01)
• Show transaction frequency data (at least 50+ onchain transactions in demo)
• Include a margin explanation: why this model would fail with traditional gas costs

This forces economic proof, not just a demo.
Tracks:

🪙 Per-API Monetization Engine 
 Build an API that charges per request using USDC, demonstrating viable per-call pricing at high frequency.



🤖 Agent-to-Agent Payment Loop
 Create autonomous agents that pay and receive value in real time, proving machine-to-machine commerce without batching or custodial control.



🧮 Usage-Based Compute Billing
 Design an application that charges per query, per compute unit, or per task with real-time settlement aligned to actual usage.



🛒 Real-Time Micro-Commerce Flow
 Build a buyer or seller experience where economic activity is triggered and settled per interaction, not per subscription.

Technology & Access
To build your project, you will use Arc (settlement layer), USDC (value), and Circle developer infrastructure such as Gateway, Nanopayments, and x402 (web-native payment standard) where available.

🔒 Required Technologies:
• Arc - All transactions settle on Arc, an EVM-compatible Layer-1.
• USDC - USDC is the native gas token and stablecoin on Arc and is used for gas fees, transfers, and smart contract interactions.
• Circle Nanopayments - Infrastructure enabling economically viable sub-cent, high-frequency transactions.

🔒 Additional Recommended Technologies:
• Circle Wallets - Recommended core wallet solution for this hackathon.
Use Circle Wallets as the primary wallet infrastructure in your submission to create secure, programmable wallets for users and agents.
• Circle Gateway for a unified USDC balance instantly accessible crosschain
• Circle Bridge Kit for moving USDC across chains

🧩 3rd Party Tools:
• x402 facilitator for verifying and submitting x402 payments

Vyper tooling and reference implementations for agentic payments and trustless AI agent infrastructure:
• Circle-titanoboa-sdk bridges Vyper + Titanoboa + x402 payments
• Vyper-agentic-payments clearly focused on agent-driven payment flows
• ERC-8004-vyper implements ERC-8004 trust layer for autonomous agents (identity, reputation, validation)


Use the tools that best fit your track and product.
